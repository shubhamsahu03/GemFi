# GemFi - AI-Powered Personal Finance Platform

## Overview
GemFi is an AI-powered financial management platform designed to help you track, analyze, and optimize your spending with real-time insights. With advanced features and intelligent automation, GemFi makes managing your finances easier, smarter, and more efficient.

## Features

### Advanced Analytics
- Get detailed insights into your spending patterns with AI-powered analytics.  
- Visualize trends and identify opportunities to save more effectively.

### Smart Receipt Scanner
- Extract data automatically from receipts using advanced AI technology.  
- Effortlessly track expenses without manual entry.

### Budget Planning
- Create and manage budgets with intelligent recommendations.  
- Stay on top of your financial goals with personalized guidance.

### Multi-Account Support
- Manage multiple accounts and credit cards in one place.  
- Seamlessly track all your financial activities in a unified dashboard.

### Multi-Currency
- Support for multiple currencies with real-time conversion.  
- Ideal for travelers or those managing international transactions.

### Automated Insights
- Get automated financial insights and recommendations.  
- Receive actionable advice to improve your financial health.

## Getting Started
1. **Sign Up**: Create an account on the GemFi platform.  
2. **Connect Accounts**: Link your bank accounts, credit cards, or manually input transactions.  
3. **Upload Receipts**: Use the Smart Receipt Scanner to automatically extract data.  
4. **Set Budgets**: Define your financial goals with the Budget Planning tool.  
5. **Explore Insights**: Leverage Advanced Analytics and Automated Insights to optimize your spending.
